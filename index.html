Data and Decision Sciences

We are a group of faculty from UNO Mathematics department with expertise in Data Science, Statistics, Operations Research. We are located in Omaha and would like to engage in communities with need for data consulting. We provide a wide range of services ranging from Statistical Modeling, Computing, Data Visualization etc. We provide undergraduate and graduate level disciplines in those areas as well, and the students are engaged in learning, working on real data processing.

Our Branches

Data Science

Data Science is the art and science of transforming raw data into deliverable data products, in order to help businesses or government agencies to make more informed decisions.

Operations Research

Operations Research is the application of advanced analytical methods to enable better decision making.

Statistics

Statistics is used in many fields, including biology, sociology, psychology, medicine, economics, quality control, and sports.
