Description of Center for Public Affairs Research

College of Public Affairs and Community Service (CPAR) is the lead agency for the Nebraska State Data Center, as a result, we have access to and the ability to organize and interpret Census data for your particular needs. In addition, we house various other federal and state data sets. We also collect original data, such as the Omaha Conditions Survey. Our staff has the skills to help you collect, analyze, interpret and visualize your data as well.
